BTW, MSEgui menu items are not components of a form and have no component 
name, they are elements of the submenu array, a descendent of  
tpersistentarrayprop.
tpersistentarrayprop and other array properties are variable lenght arrays 
which can be edited in object inspector. Persistent fields of datasets also 
are elements of an array.
