Hi,

Many editors support a function where you can delete the current line of
text with a shortcut key press. eg:  Alt+Del   or the more well known
one of Ctrl+Y

Could something like this be added to MSEide?


Workaround
----------
- Press HOME to go to start of line
- Press and hold SHIFT
- Press down arrow. Release SHIFT
- Press DEL key


A single Alt+Del would be so much quicker. ;-)
