Really exciting ! but how about GUI candies - database-state
enabling/disabling/tuning widgets etc ? For instance - disabling
action buttons until a table row is selected. Hiding/disabling whole
tab page once no more data for this page so that users won't even try
to visit the page (wasting their time & nerves),.. Reselecting
dependent DB queries & updating their bound widgets with new data...
Highlighting different data states. Sure, it involves poewreful &
easily extendable event-driven state machine.
As to me, coding this user-friendly (and error-preventing) stuff is
much harder (time consuming) that to get/put/display data to DB :)
Shorter - making program user-frendly &  error-persistent (thus better
sellable) may be much harder that just to make it functional :) What
me like MSEgui for best is that it's aimed to make polishing programs
with minimal efforts.
