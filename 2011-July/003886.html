> After I resize the widget window client rect is resized too.

The reason for not calling checkwindowrect in clientrectchanged() but on 
demand later when the window is used is that resizing a OpenGL context for 
example is expensive.
Anyway, if the question is "Please move checkwindowrect() to protected or 
public", trunk 4654 moved it to protected. Publicly available are the 
properties childrect and viewport which call checkwindowrect().
