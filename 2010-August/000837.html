> Stupid question, but what is the notation for sizety?
> 
It is in unit msegraphutils:
"
 sizety = record
            cx,cy: integer;
          end;
"
In order to build a sizety from integers in code use
"
makesize(<cxvalue>,<cyvalue>)
"
