There are two sorts of persistent fields in MSEgui. First field components
from the DBf palette which are placed into a form or datamodule, second
persistent fields which are hold in the dataset by controller.fields[n]
properties. The second kind of persistent fields is created by moving field
names from 'Fielddefs' to 'Fields'. If you want to use a field component
from the palette don't create a dataset persistent field.
So in your case move the graphic field from 'Fields' to 'Fielddefs' or
delete the according entry from controller.fields[n].
