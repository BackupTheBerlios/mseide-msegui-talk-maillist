> At designtime the tmsezquery1 is set to true, and I can navigate the
> images with the navigator and se the recordnumber in the stringgrid - no
> problem.
> 
> However, if I drop a tmsegraphicfield on the form, and try to link it to
> tmsezquery1, I get a warning that the operation cannot be performed on
> an active dataset.
> 
> I can set tmsezquery1.active to false, but when I then try to set it
> active, i get a new warning that the fields in tdbstringgrid1 cannot be

It is because if there is at least one persistent field the automatic
creation of fields for all fielddefs is switched off. Click on the ellipse
in objectinspector tmsezquery1.controller.fields.count in order to open the
fields editor, move the wanted fields from 'Fielddefs' to 'Fields'.

I don't know how graphic fields work in ZEOS, i assume that Zeos creates a
TBlobField for Image access, so TBlobField.LoadFromFile() should work.
