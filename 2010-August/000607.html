> Another Question, what are differences fkInternalCalc and fkCalculated?
> which one is faster? why only fkInternalCalc field accepted by
> tlocalindex?
> 
fkCalculated field data is not stored in the dataset but calculated by
OnCalcFields, they are not writable. fkInternalcalc fields are like fkData
fields but their values are not fetched from database, they can be
calculated once by oninternalcalcfields for example. Because their value
for every row of the dataset is known they can be used by local indexes.
