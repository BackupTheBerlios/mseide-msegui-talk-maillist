> I don't understand your reason clearly. Why onkeypress(var info:

>
OnKeypress is fired by Delphi if Windows sends a wm_char message. wm_char will 
be sent if a key press event represents an ansi character AFAIK. It is 
problematic for input methods where there is no fix relation between keys and 
characters. Because of this MSEgui has a field "chars" for keydown and keyup 
events and no OnKeypress event with a char parameter.
"
 keyeventinfoty = record
  eventkind: eventkindty;
  key,keynomod: keyty;
  chars: msestring;
  shiftstate: shiftstatesty;
  eventstate: eventstatesty;
  timestamp: longword; //usec
 end;
"
What should your "onkeypress(var info: keyeventinfoty)" do?
