The distribution of MSEide+MSEgui ist the mandatory design environment 
(mseide_msegui_src_*.zip) which is identical for all platforms and an 
optional binary (mseide_*) of the IDE specific to platform. I don't like the 
idea to mix the two parts.
Maybe mseide_msegui_src_*.zip should be renamed and the term "source" should 
be avoided?
From a marketing point of view I don't like the idea to distribute incomplete 
MSEide+MSEgui environments, for  example there is a component palette but one 
can not use the componets because the source is missing. To distribute 
precompiled *.ppu's is problematic because of possible FPC version conflicts.
