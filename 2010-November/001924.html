> Is it possible to make a MDI application?
>
IvankoB provided examples here:
http://svn.berlios.de/viewvc/msedocumenting/mse/trunk/contributed/ivankob/examples/

> Simple application with one mainform, and then a childform docked inside
> the mainform.
>
Use 'New'-'Form'-'Docking Form' for the main and the child forms. Use a 
tdockformwidget if the child should be docked at design time or use 
RightClick-'Insert Submodule' in order to create an instance of an open form. 
This is similar to the Delphi TFrame concept The dock options are 
in 'dragdock' and frame.grip_* properties.
