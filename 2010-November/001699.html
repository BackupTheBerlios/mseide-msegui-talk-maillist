Well, I did so:
start mseide.exe --macrodef=FPCDIR,w:/md/dev/freepascal/fpc/2.4.3/,
   FPCDIRLIB,{$FPCDIR}/bin/i386-win32/,
   COMPILER,${FPCDIR}/bin/i386-win32/ppc386.exe,
   DEBUGGER,W:/md/dev/freepascal/binutils/i386-win32/gdb.exe

...and it works.
