Recently I have found it better to write such "tools" or "scripts" rather
as a simple Free Pascal console application. That way the same
code/tool/script works on all platforms and no need for many *.sh, *.bat or
*.cmd files. Simple console apps work really well, they are easy to compile
(normally just 'fpc mytool.pas'), and you have the full RTL at your
disposal to do all kinds of trickery.  :-)
