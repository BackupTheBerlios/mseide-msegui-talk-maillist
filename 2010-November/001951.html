> I don't think this is correct. Pressing enter key means "execute the default

> button with the black frame which is focused or has state.as_default set.
> Pressing space key means press the focused button, releasing space key
> means fire onexecute of the focused button.

AFAIK, press space or enter for the focused button is a same action,
that is to execute onexecute event. (they are same)
But press enter for unfocused button is different than press space,
enter execute onexecute event of default button (if the form have
default button), but press space doesn't fire anything on buttons that
didn't focused. Space key only fire focused widget.
I test in KDE, Gnome and Windows, they have same method for button.
