OK, maybe I'm not understanding this correctly.

* In "Configure MSEide" dialog, I selected the form at design time, and
added a some text (the form name) in the helpcontext property.

* On the main form, I dropped a thelpcontroller and implemented a OnHelp
event handler as follows.

   procedure tmainfo.ProcessHelp(const sender: tcustomhelpcontroller;
               const helpsender: tmsecomponent; var handled: Boolean);
   begin
     writeln(Format('HelpSender.Name = %s (%s)[%s]', [HelpSender.Name,
HelpSender.ClassName, Sender.HelpContext]));
   end;

* I compiled and ran the ide. I then selected "Settings - Configure
MSEide", and immediately pressed F1. At the time I pressed F1, the "cancel"
button had focus. Below is the output I got from the OnHelp event handler.

------------------------------------
$ ./mseide.elf
X Error: BadDrawable (invalid Pixmap or Window parameter)  9
  Major opcode:  14
X Error: BadDrawable (invalid Pixmap or Window parameter)  9
  Major opcode:  14
HelpSender.Name = tbutton2 (tbutton)[]
------------------------------------

As you can see, tbutton2 (the cancel button) was passed to the OnHelp event
handler, because it is the component that had focus. But the Cancel button
doesn't have information in the HelpContext property, the "Configure
MSEide" form has HelpContext information [see attached screenshot], but it
never got to the OnHelp event handler, and I never set Handled = True. What
am I doing wrong?
