> Are you sure? IIRC default in MSEgui was "mono" and later changed to 
> "fixed" because "mono" was not defined on all systems.

In my Ubuntu 10.04 it mentions that 'mono' has been deprecated and replaced
with 'monospace', not 'fixed'.


>From the file: /etc/fonts/fonts.conf

---8<-----------8<-----------8<-----------8<-----------8<-----------
<!--
  Accept deprecated 'mono' alias, replacing it with 'monospace'
-->
	<match target="pattern">
		<test qual="any" name="family">
			<string>mono</string>
		</test>
		<edit name="family" mode="assign">
			<string>monospace</string>
		</edit>
	</match>
---8<-----------8<-----------8<-----------8<-----------8<-----------
