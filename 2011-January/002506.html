These are two widely differing approaches.
HTML is a generic formatting markup system that does _not_ specify the
exact appearance, but just general style specifications.

RTF is a word processing format, which defines the exact appearence of
every text element, including character styles and sizes and possibly line
and page breaks.

RTF was AFAIR used by Word Perfect and is a publicly available standard.
(The "RTF" implemented by Microsoft is sometimes said to be heavily
modified.)
