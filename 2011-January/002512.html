You need:
1) to draw icon (*.ico format)
2) to create its descriptor (*.rc file)
3) compile it to "*.res" with "windres" FPC utility
4) to command "use it!" in application :

{$ifdef mswindows}
    {$R podpiska-icon.res}
{$endif}

See the attchment for example.
