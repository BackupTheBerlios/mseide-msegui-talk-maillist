Uh?  Not that I know much about Lazarus's LCL etc... But you statement
seems odd, or maybe I'm not understanding what you are saying.

In fpGUI we use UTF-8 as the internal encoding. fpGUI uses resource
string constants for any text that is translatable. Currently fpGUI has
been translated to 9 languages, and I have never compiled fpGUI or my
apps with -Fcutf8, and my translations work just fine. All translation
*.po files are in UTF-8, and all resource string include files (*.inc)
which is included in fpGUI is also in UTF-8.
