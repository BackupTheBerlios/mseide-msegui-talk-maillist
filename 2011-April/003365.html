> Which fastest in encoding? asstring or tmsestringfield(xx).asmsestring?

The fastest is not to use FieldByName() but the field components in tab 
'DBf' and tmsestringfield.asmsestring. Text is stored in tmsebufdataset 
as UnicodeString (= msestring). For TField.AsString it must be converted 
to ansistring and you will loose Unicode if the system encoding is not 
utf-8.
