- activate <yourdropdowneditwidgetinstance>.dropdown.options 
deo_autodropdown, then the dropdown list will be activated with 
incremental search by entering the first character into the edit widget.
- call <yourdropdowneditwidgetinstance>.dropdown.dropdown() in 
<yourdropdowneditwidgetinstance>.onactivate (not tested).
