tsqlstringlist useses UnicodeString, the text will be converted to utf8 
if <yourconnectioninstance>.controller.options dbo_utf8 is set. As 
Wahono writes, FPC field names are 8bit strings with undefined encoding 
and should be pure ASCII, so maybe 'select 你好 as aa from table ' may 
work if your database supports non ASCII column names and the client 
encoding is utf-8. Why do you need chineese field names?
