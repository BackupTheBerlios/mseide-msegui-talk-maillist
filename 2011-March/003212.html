Remove the xx.free call it is not necessary because xx will be destroyed by 
mainfo.destroywidgets() or its owner.DestroyComponents. Additionally probably 
it is better to let TSelectOnRunTime be the owner of xx:
"
 constructor TSelectOnRunTime.Create(AOwner: TComponent); 
 
 begin
   inherited;  
 xx:=  teventwidget.create(self);
 xx.parentwidget:=twidget(AOwner);
"
