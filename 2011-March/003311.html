Named "inteldrmfb", works well on integrated Intel GPUs.

Assumes that DRM is in kernel config & GPU specific DRM setting is also in  
the config. And no framebuffer drivers should activate on loading LINUX.

Kernel boot parameters "video=VGA-1:X_RESxY_RES" & "i915.modeset=1" make  
all the magic for Intel :)

Xorg's driver for that is "intel" (OpenGL compatible) or "fbdev" (no  
OpenGL).

Helped me a lot to get native 1366x786 screen resolution.
