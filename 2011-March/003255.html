> Suppose in simple form I have a string display.. Can I call the same
> form to display data from different tasks.. Or should I have 3
> forms..?

Same form, maybe three different instances.

ie.
Form1: TSimpleForm;
Form2: TSimpleForm;
Form3: TSimpleForm;

You can then change display elements on the assigned forms. (After
they are created of course.)

Form1.Caption = 'Task 1';
Form1.Label1.Caption = 'Name';
Form2.Caption = 'Task 2'
Form2.Label1.Caption = 'Address';

Hopefully Helpful.
