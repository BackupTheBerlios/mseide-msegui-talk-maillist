It's only quicker if there's no swapping. If the application you want to
use next has been swapped out, you still have to wait.

useful for embedded applications which have to make sure they "own" the
screen at all times, i.e. are "focus stealing" - they cannot do that
beyond their desktop borders, but they _can_ do it across monitors.
