On Wednesday 02 March 2011 20:21:32 Sri Wahono wrote:
> After I update MSEGUI from last trunk and adjusting with new changes
> of msegui, Repaz could not print, but still can export to PDF and SVG.
> What is the big change in MSEGUI?
>
The GDI functions listed in gdifunctionaty have been renamed to gdi_*, later 
they will be moved in an own unit.
gui_getfont(), gui_getfonthighres(), gui_freefontdata(),              
gui_gettext16width(), gui_getchar16widths(), gui_getfontmetrics() are now GDI 
functions. The purpose is to be able to work with different GDI backends in a 
single application and to simplify the task to add new GDI backends.
