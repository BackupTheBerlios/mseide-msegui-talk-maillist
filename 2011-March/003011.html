Ah and then I realized that I have put my hand into the hornet's nest,
as the MySQL licensing is sort of tricky.

As I understand I am no allowed to include or static link the MySQL
client into my program, unless I make it GPL, meaning delivering the
source code along with the binary file, or I use/purchase an oem license
from MySQL, or I sell the whole enchilada meaning Server etc. to my
customer.

On the other hand, if the customer himself installs the server, I do not
have to care about his licensing, if he is providing the library from
the server installation - is that correct understud?

Zeoslib should not be a problem, as that is LGPL.

All I wanted to obtain was to make a simple solution - sigh.
