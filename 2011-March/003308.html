Hi,
SVN trunk has some new MSEgui DB functions:
- Savepoint support (tested on Firebird only up to now).
- Macros in SQL-text.
- Connector components for params and tsqlresult columns.
- datasource and fieldname properties in tmseparam.
- "asid" property in tmse*intfield and tmseparam (-1 if NULL).

I hope you will find the new functions useful.
