What local storage of news issues do you have? I always tell
Thunderbird to store news locally - that way I can do full message
body searches or read messages offline.

Thunderbird (enabling offline reading / local storage)
  * Right click on subscribed newsgroup
  * Select "properties" in the popup menu
  * Select the last tab: Syncronization (I think it was called Offline
in older versions)
  * Tick the "offline use" checkbox
  * click the Download button.

Now you have all existing message available and locally stored. Any
new messages will automatically be store locally too.
