Am Dienstag 10 Mai 2011, 16:48:38 schrieb Christian Nobel:
> Is it possible to make multiselect in a twidgetgrid.
> 
> If so, which options have to be set?
> 
The select options (co_focusselect, co_mouseselect, co_keyselect, 
co_multiselect, co_resetselectonexit, co_rowselect) are in tdatacol.options, 
the default values in t*grid.datacols.options.
