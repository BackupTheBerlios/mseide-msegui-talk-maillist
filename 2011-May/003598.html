On Thursday 12 May 2011 07:58:06 IvankoB wrote:
> So, lookup buffers are preferrable when:
>
> 1) it provides more than one tdb*(edit|disp)lb with data;
> 2) there're data editing dropdowns bound to these lookupbuffers;
> 3) there're needs to use their "lookup<type>" methods.
>
> That for any serious application, correct ?
>
No, all these can be done with the new tdb*(edit|disp)db direct data access by 
local index too. Lookupbuffers are preferable if the lookupdata does not 
change and the lookup dataset can be closed after loading the lookupbuffer. 
For that case a tsqllookupbuffer can be used too BTW.
