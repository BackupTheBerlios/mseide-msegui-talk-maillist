> Where is tactivator.active set to true? In which form/module is it  
> located?

Both activator (design-time = initially active) & its client queriers are  
placed on a datamodule whose instance is created in overriden constructor  
of a form/report:

//--------------------
constructor tform18viewfo.Create(aowner: tcomponent);
begin
	dmf18moacquire;
	inherited;
end;

destructor tform18viewfo.Destroy;
begin
	inherited;
	dmf18morelease;
end;
//--------------------
