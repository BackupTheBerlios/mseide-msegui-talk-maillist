No, it is not very difficult nor time consuming. Start your current
MSEide, 'Project'-'Open', select apps/ide/mseide.prj from the trunk SVN
checkout, press F9. Should be done in less than 5 minutes. ;-)
Compiling MSEide from commandline isn't difficult either.
For Linux:
"
ppc386
apps/ide/mseide.pas -Fulib/common/kernel/i386-linux -Fi/lib/common/kernel -Fulib/common/*
"
For Windows:
"
ppc386
apps/ide/mseide.pas -Fulib/common/kernel/i386-win32 -Fi/lib/common/kernel -Fulib/common/*
"
