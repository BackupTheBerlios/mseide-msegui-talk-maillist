Christian Nobel wrote:

> 
> If opening a picture and saving it again reduces the filesize on the new
> file compared to the old dramatically.
> 
> However the resolution in pixels is the same, but looking closer to the
> pictures makes me believe that the color depth is reduced from 32bit to
> 24 - is this assumption correct?
> 
MSEgui bitmaps are 8/8/8 bit RGB. Does your file have more than 8bit per
color channel? It is more likely another compression level. Default for jpg
write is 75, the possible range is 0..100. Example for compressionlevel 10:
"
 timage1.bitmap.WriteTofile(tfiledialog1.controller.filename,'jpg',[10]);
"
