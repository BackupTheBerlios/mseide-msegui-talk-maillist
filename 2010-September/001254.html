I don't know if I have understood your question correctly, but in
Lazarus I would do something like (used in a program where I have
columns and rows with panels as placeholders, on which there are touch
screen buttons):

.
.
Form1.BorderStyle:=bsNone;
Form1.WindowState:=wsMaximized;
.
.
Bwidth:=(Form1.Width div Columnnbrs)-spacing;
Bheight:=(Form1.Height div Rawnbrs)-spacing;
for y:=0 to Rawnbrs-1 do
begin
  for x:=0 to Columnnbrs-1 do
  begin
    if (y*Columnnbrs+x)<BtnNumbers then
    begin
      mypanels[y*Columnnbrs+x].Height:=Bheight;
      mypanels[y*Columnnbrs+x].Width:=Bwidth;
      mypanels[y*Columnnbrs+x].left:=x*(Bwidth+spacing)+(spacing div 2);
      mypanels[y*Columnnbrs+x].top:=y*(Bheight+spacing)+(spacing div 2);
    end;
  end;
end;
.
.

How this have to be done in MSE I do not have the faintest idea of, as
it probably will take a week to guess the similar behavior (my god how
code insertion/completion could make ones life easier).
