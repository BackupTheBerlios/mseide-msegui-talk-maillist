Ivanko B wrote:

> It seems that bounds.cy of subwidgets of widgetgrid aren't inherited,
> in form inheritance.

Works for me. You propably changed the row height of the descendant.

BTW, the height of grid widgets is defined by the cell height. Widgets with
optionswidget ow_autoscale set propagate their ow_fonglypheight or
ow_fontlineheight height adjustment to the grid rowheight -> only one of
the grid widgets in a row should have ow_autoscale set.
