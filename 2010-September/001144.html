"virtual" is the modifier for the base method, "override" can be used in a
descendant class. Example:

var
 list1: tdatalist;
 mstr1: msestring;
[...]
 list1:= trealdatalist.create;
 list1.count:= 200;
 mstr1:= list1.getastext(123); //calls trealdatalist.getastext.
