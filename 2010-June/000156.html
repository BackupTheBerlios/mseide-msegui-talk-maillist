There are no _technical_ problems with mailing lists - it's just that the
medium _email_ isn't meant for public conversation, so this is really a
misuse of the medium.
The internet provides a public conversation / discussion medium (or you may
call it channel) by means of the usenet, running on the nntp protocol.
This should be used for this type of correspondence, and that's why _some_
people have an aversion towards mailing lists.
