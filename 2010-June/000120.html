Please NOTE the different email addresses for the mailing list.  This
was all mentioned in the "welcome to MSEide...." email, after you
subscribed.



To post to this list (the general public will see these emails too),
send your email to:
   mseide-msegui-talk at lists.berlios.de


You can make adjustments to your account settings via email by sending
a message to:
  MSEide-MSEgui-talk-request at lists.berlios.de

                                  ^^^^^^
Note the "-request" suffix.


The last email address is the one you actually want to send those
messages to. That means you talk to Mailman (the mailing list program)
directly, and can adjust your account settings. Currently you are
posting to the public mailing list which everybody can see, and not
actually talking to the mailing list program (mailman).
