ru_RU.UTF8 has "nbs" as thousandseparator, KDE's regional settings does not
change what nl_langinfo reports in a console program AFAIK.
In order to change the setting application wide use
"
 defaultformatsettingsmse.thousandseparator:= <yourseparator>;
"
In order to change the settings for a single or group of conversions only:
"
var
 myformatsettings: tformatsettingsmse;
[...]
 myformatsettings:=  defaultformatsettingsmse;
 myformatsettings.thousandseparator:= <yourseparator>;
 <stringvalue>:= formatfloatmse(<value>,<format>,myformatsettings);
"
