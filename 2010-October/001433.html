> function scaleexp10(const value: currency; const exp: integer): currency;
> var                                                             ^^^^^^^^
>  int1: int64;
         ^^^^^
Sadly, you didn't tell _what_ error you got - the two places _where_ it
occurs seem to signify that the types of "currency" and "int64" on ARM
aren't exactly corresponding to their x86 counterparts. Possibly "currency"
on ARM is smaller than "int64", so a direct cast cannot be made, becuse it
would overflow the variable's memory space.
Another possible issue might be that, on x86, "currency" AFAIK is a
_floting point_ type (in the sense that it is processed by the FPU), while
this might not be the case on ARM machines.

> Any MSEgui users have success compile MSEgui for Android?
