The fade is made by the imagelist with colormask=true, AKA alphachannel. In
MSEgui a colormask alphachannel has the components R,G and B -> every color
component has its own transparency.
The tiled background of mainfo.container.face is a combination of a fade and
a pixmap with monochrome mask and semi-transparency by
face.image.transparency. tmaskedbitmap.transparency also has RGB
components. Combining colormask and transparency value is not supported.
In order to show some more possibilities I added a semi transparent edit
widget with font gloss and shadow, rev. 117.
