The question is, why take all that unneccessary crap of the "standard"
solution that's not needed for the given task and not just build something

chance to grow beyond close limits, a do-it-yourself approach can be very
rewarding (this applies to equipment controlling microcontrollers much more
than to PC-based "virtual world" resident applications).
