>Then how to do it (after-inrefactive-edit modification to a record
>before writting its new contents to the DB ) correctly ?

There's a "solution" found to override OnApplyRecUpdate:

//-----------------

procedure trefoperatorviewfo.operatorapplyrecupdate(const sender:
tmsesqlquery;
               const updatekind: TUpdateKind; var asql: AnsiString;
               var done: Boolean);
begin
  sender.fieldbyname('office_code').aswidestring:=
dmmainmo.fldWPOfficeCode.aswidestring;
  inherited applyrecupdate(sender,updatekind,asql,done);
end;

//------------

Seems working. Is it ugly ?
