On Tuesday, 18. May 2010 12.48:07 Graeme Geldenhuys wrote:
> Hi,
>
> What is tactcomponent's purpose?  Does the name mean "action
> component", "activator component" or something else?
>
tactcomponent is a component which can be a client of tactivator. Additionally 
it has the release() procedure to be used in own event handlers.

> Also what is an "activator" (as in tactivator) is MSEgui?

tactivator is used in order to activate or deactivate it's clients in a 
definable sequence. Example are DB applications where datasets must be 
activated in a fix order. Also used at design time as "mainswitch" for a 
group of components.
