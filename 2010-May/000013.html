Hi Martin,

Just a suggestion to get the Mailman mailing list working as expected.
Currently when you reply to a message it goes directly to that person,
and NOT to the mailing list. No the expected behaviour. To fix this
change the following setting:


The the Mailman Admin pages:

* General Options
   - Where are replies to list messages directed?
     Change this from "Poster"  ->   "This List"


Now replies to message will go back to the mailing list. I had to
figure this one out as well when I setup the fpGUI and tiOPF mailing
lists. :)
