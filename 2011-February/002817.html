> How to autofit width in column(like in excel) in tdbstringgrid by
> double click in column(when look double-headed arrow)?

There is an "autocellsize" option in drawinfo for drawcell() procedure, used 
for individual row heights only up to now. A similar function for columns is 
already on wishlist. At the moment you must calculate the col width yourself.
