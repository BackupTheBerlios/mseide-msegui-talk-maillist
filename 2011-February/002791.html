You can do

tmsesqlquery.sql.add('select * from table1 where table1.id=:idvalue');

and then for example..

tmsesqlquery.params.parambyname('id').asinteger :=
inttostr(tstringedit.value);

but better use tintegeredit instead of tstringedit resulting in

tmsesqlquery.params.parambyname('id').asinteger :=  tintegeredit.value; //
replace tmsesqlquery and tintegeredit with the widget names
