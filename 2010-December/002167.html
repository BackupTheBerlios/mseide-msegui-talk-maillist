How me build MSE:

- go the MSE work copy
- svn update
- svn export .\ <target>
- runing the below CMD-script in the export target directory:

//-----------------------

-Fulib\common\kernel\i386-win32 -Fi\lib\common\kernel -dmse_with_ifi

fpc -B tools\bmp2pas.pas   -Fulib\common\*
-Fulib\common\kernel\i386-win32 -Fi\lib\common\kernel

fpc -B tools\form2pas.pas  -Fulib\common\*
-Fulib\common\kernel\i386-win32 -Fi\lib\common\kernel
