> Is it for separate widgets and components in design form?

Submodules in MSEgui are the Delphi TFrame equivalent. In contrast to Delphi 
it is possible in MSEgui to use any form as submodule source. Now it is 
possible to use datamodules for nonvisual submodules and it is possible to 
insert datamodule-submodules into datamodules.
