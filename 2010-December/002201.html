I don't know, but if you want to make some sort of login mechanism, I
have done like this:

procedure tloginfo.projectlogin(const sender: TObject);
begin
	application.createform(tmainfo,mainfo);	
	loginfo.visible:=false;
end;

And remember both forms have to have:

tmainfo = class(tmainform)
tloginfo = class(tmainform)

fo_main=true and fo_terminateonclose=true
