On 01/06/2011 15:53, Martin Schreiber wrote:
> https://bugzilla.novell.com/show_bug.cgi?id=690766
> Did you try the "EXAPixmaps" "off" option?

No I didn't, because  I thought the bug is not  related to my problem. I
never got any corrupted display/painting as your attachment showed.

But,  I just  added that  option  to my  xorg.conf and  removed the  -ns
parameter. I seems my issue was  related after all! Now even without the
-ns parameter, MSEide is back to full speed. :-D

PS:
You are not the only one that has noticed issues in X11 display drivers.
fpGUI also has  issues with the open source ATI  driver. Problem is gone
when I use vesa, fbdev or fglrx  drivers. It seems they should ask us to
test display  drivers with our custom drawn toolkits *before*  they make
new releases. :-)
