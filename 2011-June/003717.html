Ah, that did the trick!!! :-) I remember reading somewhere that there is
a command line option to disable skinning/theming support. A quick  read

> BTW, why don't You use verdor-supllied (proprietary) drivers ?

The latest ATI/AMD  drives doesn't support the Radeon  Mobility 9600 any
more. :-( I have also found  that the newer open-source ATI drivers have
sufficient speed  and features  that the closed  source drivers  are not
needed any  more.  I  don't really  play games, but  now with  the later
open-source  ati  drivers, even  my  OpenGL  screensavers run  perfectly
smooth.
